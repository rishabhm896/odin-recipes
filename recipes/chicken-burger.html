Chicken burger

Description

A chicken burger makes for a quick midweek meal or weekend BBQâ€™s with friends! Crispy seasoned chicken breast, topped with mandatory melted cheese, piled onto soft rolls with onion, avocado, lettuce, tomato and garlic mayo.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Bread crumbs
  • Onion
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Mix the chicken, half of the crumbs, onion, egg, and seasonings in a bowl.
  2. Shape the mixture into oval-shaped patties.
  3. Coat the patties into the remaining bread crumbs.
  4. Cook the patties until they are deeply brown on both sides.
